What Is This?

Are you not entertained by the news? Here's a little thingy that makes reading the news more fun. It substitutes certain phrases in an article with more amusing alternatives.

The idea comes from a particular series of comics from xkcd.

Link 1 Link 2 Link 3

How Does It Work?

  1. Copy and paste an article URL into the text box.
  2. Enter or click the button to process the article.
  3. Have fun reading the resulting article with substitutions!
